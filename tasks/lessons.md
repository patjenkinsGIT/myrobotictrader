**Mistake:** N/A (initial setup)

**Mistake:** N/A (initial setup)

### 2026-02-27 — Communication Clarity

**Mistake:** Said "zero posts published" when the MCP topic tracker showed "Posts Generated: 0" per topic. The site has ~40+ blog posts — the counter only tracks whether a specific watchlist topic has been turned into a post yet.

**Context:** Pat has been building content for months. Implying the site has no content is dismissive of that work and erodes trust.
